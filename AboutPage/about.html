About Us

Accelerate Classroom Science.

Hands-On Primary Workshops to boots Science Concepts.

Our team of academic specialists truly care about connecting young boffins to the world of science and its wonders. We conduct non-hazardous offline science activities and experiments and online workshops with impressive demonstrations, safe hand on experiments that prove to be amazing take aways for primary and kindergarten learners. The purpose of these workshops is to revolutionize classroom science learning. Our talented workshop demonstrators are experienced in teaching science in Primary school as well as at early childhood learning centers. Some are qualified teachers and some are trained professionals in specific science streams such as physical, life, and earth sciences.

FAQ

Who leads the Science Workshops?
Our talented workshop demonstrators are experienced in teaching science in Primary school and early childhood learning centers. Some are qualified teachers and some are trained scientists with a specific science streams such as physical, life or earth science.
What is included in the cost?
A personalized presenter for your class or group, setup and cleanup of the venue, all materials and equipment, a certificate for each child, a PD certificate for each teacher/supervisor, and further DIY activity ideas.
Are your workshops safe?
Rest assured, our sessions are completely safe. We only choose the safest of activities without compromising on learning
Where do you operate?
We currently operate within primary schools and in Early Childhood Learning settings in Dubai,Sharjah, and Ajman. We offer a personal teaching service just for your grade. We also conduct Holiday programs at different locations in Sharjah, Ajman and Dubai and we offer group sessions. Our online workshops are available. Call us for more information.
How do I prepare for the session?
We need minimal space and certain items that will be informed to you beforehand.
-Six clear tables for students to work at;
-A presentation table and floor space to accommodate the class in front;
-The use of a rubbish bin and electrical power point;
-Access to the venue 30 minutes prior and at the end of the session for setting-up and cleaning-up;
-Parking as close as possible to the venue;
-Some workshops require access to a tap and basin.
-We ask that teachers help with the supervision of sessions.
You might be surprised with what you learn too – a perfect professional development opportunity!
What topics do you offer?
Our classroom workshops align with leading international curriculums. Our topics cover physical, life, earth and space , simultaneously linked to math, technology, arts,and chemistry sciences. If there is a topic of interest not listed on our website, please inquire as we are skilled at tailoring programs based on specific needs and requirements.
