Employment opportunities at Brilliant Boffin

Brilliant Boffins are always on the lookout for experienced teachers to join our vibrant and dynamic team delivering specialised science programs . This is a great opportunity to take control of your lifestyle and nominate the days you wish to work. You will receive full training on how to present our sessional science programs, with everything you need provided. Working with us gives you the chance to specialise, as well as be creative, dramatic, energetic and enthusiastic as you inspire students to take an interest in science.

Attractive and flexible sessional work. Part time only.

Requirements

Experience of working with primary age students
Passionate about science
Good communication skills.

Applications

Please send your application via email to boffinbrilliant@gmail.com
